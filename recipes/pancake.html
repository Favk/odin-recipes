Pancakes

Description

Today, we'll be making pancakes. You will need to make the batter and then fry on a thin layer of oil

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk (plant based substitue if you're lactose intolerant)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Water
  • Maple syrup
  • Fruits and berries

Steps / Procedures:

  1. First, sieve the dry ingredients to remove any dirst or clumps present in them
  2. Put the dry ingredients in a bowl for mixing
  3. Mix dry ingredientswell before adding the eggs and milk
  4. For the eggs, separate the egg white from the egg yolk. Whisk the egg whites till solid
  5. Add the egg yolks and milk into the mixture of the flour and sugar
  6. Once thoroughy mixed, add a few drops of vanilla extract into the batter
  7. Then fold the solid egg whites into the mixture with a spatula
