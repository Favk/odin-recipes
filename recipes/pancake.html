Pancakes

Description

Today, we'll be making pancakes. You will need to make the batter and then fry on a thin layer of oil

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Milk (plant based substitue if you're lactose intolerant)
  • Vanilla extract
  • Maple syrup or honey
  • Fruits and berries

Steps / Procedures:

  1. First, sieve the dry ingredients to remove any dirt or clumps present in them
  2. Put the dry ingredients in a bowl for mixing
  3. Mix dry ingredients thoroughly before adding the eggs and milk
  4. For the eggs, separate the egg white from the egg yolk. Whisk the egg whites till solid
  5. Add the egg yolks and milk into the mixture of the flour and sugar
  6. Once thoroughy mixed, add a few drops of vanilla extract into the batter
  7. Then fold the solid egg whites mixture into the batter with a spatula
  8. Once mixed well, put a pan on the stove, put a tablespoon of butter to melt on medium heat
  9. Once the butter is melted, put a spoon of the batter into the pan, flip every 3 minutes till brown on both sides
  10. Once you're done you can start plating. You stack the pancakes on each other, put some syrup on the top of the pancake and add your fruits as in the picture above.
  11. Serve with hot chocolate or coffee

Enjoy!
