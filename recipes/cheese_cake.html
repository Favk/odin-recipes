American Cheese Cake

Decription

A dessert consisting of a creamy filling usually containing cheese baked in a pastry or pressed-crumb shell

Ingredients

    For the Crust
  • 170g graham cracker crumbs
  • 2 Tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brown or white sugar
  • 7 tablespoons of melted butter
    • Cheese Cake
  • 910g of softened cream cheese
  • 200g sugar
  • 160g sour cream
  • 1 and half tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • an eigth teaspoon of salt
  • 4 large eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 325F or 160C
  2. Prepare graham cracker crust by combining graham cracker crumbs, sugar and brown sugar and stirring well. Add metled butter and use a fork to combine ingredients well
  3. Pour crumbs into springform pan and press firmly into the bottom and up the sides of the pan. Set aside
  4. In a large bowl using a hand mixer add cream cheese and stir till smooth and creamy
  5. Add sugar and stir again till creamy
  6. Add sour cream, vanilla extract and salt and stir until well combined
  7. With mixer on low speed, gradually add lightly beaten, eggs one at a time, stirring until each egg is just incorporated
  8. Pour cheesecake batter into preapred pan. To prevent leaks place pan on a cookie sheet that has been lined with foil
  9. Transfer to your oven and bake on 325F for about 75 minutes
