Nigerian Jollof Rice

Description

Making a rice that is cooked in tomato and pepper puree. Boiled with chicken or beef broth.

Served with a side of meat and fried plantains

Ingredients needed

  • Rice
  • Chicken or beef broth
  • Your choice of meat
  • Unripe plantains
  • Bell peppers
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Spices such as thyme, rosemary, salt, bay leaves, chicken or beef bouillon etc.
